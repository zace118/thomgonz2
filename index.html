Thomas Gonzalez

Recently, I've stepped into the role of Director of Marching Percussion with the Pride of Arizona. I've been on staff since 2013 focusing primarily with the snares, but gradually expanding my scope to the entire battery ensemble.

I marched with The Pride of Arizona (or POA as we hip kids call it) from 2008 until 2012. In the off season, I kept up my chops and development with a local Winter Guard International group called Fusion Percussion. I marched there from 2009 until 2012.
